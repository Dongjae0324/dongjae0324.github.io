Dongjae Jeon


I am an incoming master's student in Computer Science at Yonsei University, joining the Artificial Intelligence and Information Systems Lab under the supervision of Professor Albert No.

My research interests lie in the domain of safe and reliable AI, with a particular focus on generative models . I am also interested in developing efficient AI system. Previously, I have conducted research on Continual Learning in the context of computer vision. My primary goal is to deepen our understanding of how machines perceive and to create methods that enhance their reliability.
